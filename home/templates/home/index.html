Who are the Internation Education?

We are a team of bilingual professionals who specialise in the educatoin process within the United Kingdom. We have put over 3000+ students in mutlitple different univesities across the country while assisting them in finding the appropriate accomodation that would suit there needs and requirements.
The main goal that drives us to be where we are today, is to ensure that all students have the opportunity to a better education of their choosing and and not allow english to be the barrier.
